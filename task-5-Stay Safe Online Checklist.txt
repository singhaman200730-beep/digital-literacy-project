 Stay Safe Online Checklist
??? Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips
* Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone 
* Always verify customer care numbers from official websites 
* Do not click on suspicious links or unknown messages 
* Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts 
* Use strong and unique passwords for different apps 
* Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions 
* Regularly update your apps and phone software 
?? UPI / Financial Safety Tips
* Remember: You don’t need to enter a PIN to receive money 
* Always check payment requests carefully before approving 
?? Report Cybercrime
* Report immediately on National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) 
* Call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 



Short Reflection:
While researching this topic, what surprised me the most was how simple tricks can lead to serious financial loss. Many scams do not involve hacking but instead rely on manipulating people into making mistakes, such as entering their UPI PIN unknowingly. It made me realize that awareness is more important than technical knowledge in preventing cybercrime.
As a result, one habit I will personally change is being more cautious while handling online payments. I will always double-check payment requests and verify sources before taking any action. I will also avoid trusting unknown customer care numbers found online. This experience has taught me that staying alert and informed is the best way to stay safe in the digital world.
