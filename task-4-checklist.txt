Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist
? Do’s
* Think before you post anything online 
* Maintain a respectful tone in comments and messages 
* Protect your personal information and privacy 
* Verify information before sharing it 
* Use social media for learning and positive engagement 

? Don’ts
* Don’t share fake news or unverified content 
* Don’t post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content 
* Don’t overshare personal details like address or phone number 
* Don’t engage in online arguments or trolling 
* Don’t misuse others’ photos or content without permission
